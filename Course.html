Our Courses

Courses We Offer

MumoTech Robotics & Coding College is a forward-thinking institution founded by Shadrack Mumo to empower learners across Africa. We specialize in coding, robotics, and computer hardware engineering — giving learners and tutors the hands-on skills to create, code, and construct technology from scratch.

Pragramming & Web Development

Learn how to code using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python. Master website design and software development, from beginner to professional level, with hands-on projects and real coding practice.

Robotics & Artificial Intelligence

Explore the world of robotics and A.I. Build and program robots that think, move, and respond. Learn automation, sensors, and smart systems that power modern technology.

Computer Hardware & Engineerings

Discover how computers work by building one from scratch. Learn about components, assembly, troubleshooting, and maintenance — gaining full understanding of computer hardware systems.

Our Facilities

At MumoTech Institute, we provide a modern learning environment that blends technology, innovation, and creativity. Our facilities are designed to help learners explore, experiment, and excel in coding, robotics, and computer engineering.

Modern Computer Labs

Our fully equipped computer labs give learners hands-on experience in programming, robotics, and hardware assembly using up-to-date tools and devices.

Robotics Innovation Studio

We provide a dedicated robotics workspace where learners design, build, and program robots — sparking creativity and real-world problem-solving.

Instructor Training Rooms

Our training rooms host tutor development sessions, equipping educators with modern digital teaching skills and coding knowledge to inspire the next generation.
